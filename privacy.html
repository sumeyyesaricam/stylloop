Privacy Policy for Stylloop

Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Stylloop ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application ("Service") and our integrations with third-party platforms, specifically the TikTok API.

1. Information We Collect

Stylloop operates primarily as a mobile application utility. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. Depending on how you interact with our app, we may process the following types of information:

  • TikTok Integration Data: When you authenticate via TikTok Login, we access certain information provided through the TikTok API based on the permissions (scopes) you grant. This may include your TikTok public profile information (username, display name, avatar) and specific content actions required for the app's functionality (such as reading media lists or publishing content as explicitly initiated by you).
  • Device-Specific Information: The app operates on your device and may access temporary local storage for caching assets, configuration configurations, and user preferences.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected from you strictly for the following purposes:

  • To enable third-party platform integrations, allowing you to seamlessly share, manage, or interact with video content via the TikTok API.
  • To provide, maintain, and optimize the features and core functionality of the Stylloop application.
  • To comply with legal obligations and the platform policies of our third-party provider integrations.

Note: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data or TikTok user data to any third-party advertisers or data brokers.

3. Data Storage and Retention

We believe in data minimization. Content authorization tokens received via the TikTok API are stored securely on your local device or processed in real-time transiently to complete the requested actions. We do not maintain long-term databases storing your private platform interactions or credentials on external servers.

4. Third-Party Platform Compliance

By using the TikTok integration features within Stylloop, you acknowledge and agree that your data handling is also governed by TikTok's official policies. You can consult TikTok's Privacy Policy for more details on how they protect your data.

5. User Rights & Data Deletion Request (TikTok Data)

You have full control over your data. You can revoke Stylloop's access to your TikTok account at any time directly through your TikTok account settings under "Security and login" > "Manage app access".

If you wish to request the complete deletion of any lingering information associated with your account within our app operations, please contact us at our support email provided below. Upon receiving your request, we will permanently purge all related data within 48 hours.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or operational and legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed.
